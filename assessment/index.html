Questions

Everything you need to know

Is this actually free?
Yes, completely. No credit card, no trial, no catch. The assessment and your full personalised report are free.
How long does it take?
Most people complete it in 4–6 minutes. There are 24 questions across 6 dimensions. Your results are shown immediately after you enter your email.
Is this financial advice?
No. This is an educational tool only. It is designed to help you understand your own readiness — not to replace a mortgage broker, financial adviser, or conveyancer. Always consult a licensed professional before making property decisions.
Who is this for?
Australian first-home buyers who are actively preparing to buy, or who are already searching and want clarity on where their gaps are. Most useful for buyers 3–18 months away from purchase.
What happens to my email address?
Your email is used to send your report. We will not spam you or sell your data. You can unsubscribe from any follow-up communications at any time.
Is the assessment Australian-specific?
Yes. Every question, insight, and action is built around the Australian property market — including government schemes, state-specific processes, and Australian lender behaviour.
